Uses

The variety of uses for aloe vera can be divided up into two main categories.

  1. Over thousands of years, aloe vera has been used as a treatment to many ailments ranging in severity.Including:
    • Psoriasis
    • Dandruff
    • Acne
    • Anal Fissures
    • Herpes Sores
    • Sunburn

    Modern doctors of western medecine disagree with aloe's usage to treat things like diabetes cancer, as there is little research to support it being useful in those applications. Note, it is recommended that anyone who has an allergy to onions and/or garlic refrain from topical usage of aloe vera, as the plants similarities may cause an allegic reaction.

  2. Beauty
    3. Topical usasge of aloe vera isn't limited just to medical use. As far back as the Ancient Egyptians, aloe vera has been popular for it's versatile beauty uses.
    • Hair Growth
    • Weight Loss
    • Dry Skin Treatment
    • Body Detoxification
